Cold Brew

Cold brew coffee makes for a strong, full-bodied, iced coffee beverage. There are many different vessles to make cold brew in, this method can be used interchangably on any of them.

Step 1. Grind coffee on french press setting.
Step 2. Dump grounds into brewer.
Step 3. Add INT UNITS cold tap water.
Step 4. Stir ensuring to get all grounds wet.
Step 5. At 16-18 hours, stir grounds one more time and filter out coffee into a large carafe.
Step 6. Diltute with water. Recipe is generally 1:1 coffee:water, but it is recommended to add the water to best taste.
Store in refrigerater. Good for 7-10 Days. Serve with Ice.
